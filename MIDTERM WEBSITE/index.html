About Us

Pritong Manok ni San Pedro

Talagang mapapaakyat ka sa sarap!

At Pritong Manok ni San Pedro, we aren't just serving fried chicken, we're serving up a taste of tradition. Our story is rooted in family, heritage, and a recipe passed down for generations. We take pride in being a family-owned business, where every piece of chicken is a testament to our heritage. We believe in the simple pleasures of gathering around a table, sharing a meal, and creating memories that last a lifetime. So, come on in, savor the taste of tradition, and experience the magic of Pritong Manok ni San Pedro. We guarantee it'll be a flavor that sticks with you, long after the last bite.
